Island Sanctuary Workshop Planner

For the most part, start by copying the current (or predicted, if you're planning out the next season) values for popularity and supply into the Supply & Demand tab. Once that's done, the Product Tree tab can be used to see a list of products and what can chain with them. Expanding a product shows what chains with it, and can be used to plan out a workshop schedule.

Note that the supply changes between cycles (real-world days) - the "demand shift" combines with the products built to determine the new supply with the next cycle.
